Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Daily Deals

How does the product rotation work?

Daily Deals randomly selects a specified number of products from your chosen source (collections, product types, or tags), applies a unique tag, and adds them to a dedicated smart collection. On each scheduled cycle, the previous selection is cleared and new products are randomly chosen.

Can I use this just for collection rotation without discounts?

Yes! You can set the discount percentage to 0%, which means products rotate into the featured collection with no price change. This is perfect for "Staff Picks", "Featured Products", or any curated section you want to keep fresh automatically.

What happens to products when they rotate out?

When products rotate out, their unique tag is removed and they return to their normal state. No permanent changes are made to your product data. It's completely safe and reversible.

Can I exclude specific products or collections?

Yes. You can set exclusion rules by collection, product type, or tag. Any products matching your exclusion criteria will never appear in the rotation, even if they belong to an eligible source.

What schedule frequencies are available?

You can schedule rotations hourly, daily, weekly, or set custom intervals like every 2 hours, every 3 days, etc. You can also set optional start and end dates for seasonal campaigns.

Will this slow down my store?

No. The rotation runs as a background process on our servers. It tags and untags products via the Shopify API. There is no script or code snippet injected into your storefront theme, so your store speed is completely unaffected.

Is there a limit on the number of deals or products?

No limits at all. All features are included in the single plan with no restrictions on the number of scheduled deals, products, or rotations.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. There are no contracts or commitments. You can cancel directly from your Shopify admin at any time. You also get a full 7-day free trial to test everything before being charged.
